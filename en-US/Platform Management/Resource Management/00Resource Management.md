Resource management is used to include servers, databases, and other resources into the management platform or to remove them from the management platform. When adding a resource, the following steps should be followed:

1. Add the server that hosts YashanDB in [Server Management](Server Management).
2. Add the YashanDB database instance in [Database Management](Database Management).

Before performing resource configuration, please ensure that YashanDB is correctly installed and deployed on the server.
