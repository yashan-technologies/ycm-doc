The management platform will conduct real-time monitoring and information aggregation of tasks or jobs. You can perform [Task Management](Task Management) or [Job Management](Job Management) on the corresponding list page.

- Task: Includes key operations such as resource (servers, databases) management, ad-hoc inspections, ad-hoc backups, and time synchronization settings.

- Job: Includes [ad-hoc backup](../../../Database O&M Guide/Backup Management/Backup and Restore), [periodic backup](../../../Database O&M Guide/Backup Management/Backup Policy Settings), and [ad-hoc or periodic inspection](../../../Database O&M Guide/Diagnosis Optimization/Inspection) operations.
