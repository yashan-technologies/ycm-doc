This section provides guidance and examples for the installation, deployment, and uninstallation process of the management platform.

## Installing the Management Platform

Please follow the order of operations below:

1. Before officially running the installation program, please read the [Preparation](Preparation) document and configure accordingly as instructed.

2. Execute the installation; the management platform offers two installation methods:

   - [Command Line Installation](Installation via CLI): For command line installation, you need to log in to the server. All parameters will be configured manually during the process. For deployment commands, refer to [ycm-init](../../Reference/Tools Introdution/ycm-init).
   - [Graphical Installation](Installation via GUI): After starting the web service on the server, users can enter the URL in their local browser and follow the prompts on the webpage for graphical installation.

3. After a successful installation, please read the [Initial Environment](Initial Environment) document to understand various initial information about the management platform, and make configurations and adjustments as needed.

## Uninstalling the Management Platform

To uninstall the management platform, you must first remove the managed resources, then perform the [uninstallation](Uninstall) operation on the server.
